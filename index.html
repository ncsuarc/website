ARC - Students creating awesome systems

Real world experience building and flying excellent systems

NCSU MAE Undergraduate Student Organization

Design

We design our airframe, electrical systems, and software from the ground up. Our system is tailored to our mission.

Build

We build our system from our designs. Students get experience facing real-world challenges dealing with the implementation of mechanical, electrical, and software systems.

Compete

Our careful design efforts and hard work during construction pay off when we go to compete. We focus on the AUVSI SUAS competition, and we are the only team to compete in it every year. As far as whether or not we are successful, our accomplishments speak for themselves.

Recent Accomplishments

2nd Place - AUVSI SUAS 2013

  • First year competing with Fenrir UAS
  • Autonomous waypoint navigation, search, and dynamic retasking
  • Five targets identified; high accuracy positioning
  • Actionable, remote, and electronic intelligence objectives met
  • Competed against 35 (FIXME) teams from the US, Canada, India, Turkey, and Spain

6th Place - AUVSI SUAS 2012

  • Final year competing with ARCWulf UAS
  • Difficulties with takeoff and the autopilot delayed the mission
  • Autonomous search and dynamic retasking
  • Eight targets identified and remote intelligence objective met
  • Competed against 32 (FIXME) teams from the US, Canada, India, and FIXME

2nd Place - AUVSI SUAS 2011

  • Autonomous takeoff, waypoint navigation, search, and dynamic retasking
  • FIXME targets identified
  • Actionable intelligence objective met
  • Competed against 26 teams from the US, Canada, India, and FIXME

1st Place - AUVSI SUAS 2010

  • Autonomous takeoff, waypoint navigation, search, and dynamic retasking
  • Eight targets identified
  • Actionable intelligence objective met
  • Good Samaritan Award
  • Competed against 25 teams from the US, Canada, India, and FIXME
